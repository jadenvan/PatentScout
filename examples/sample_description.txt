Title: Foldable Solar Charger with Integrated Battery Pack

Description:
A portable solar panel that folds into a compact case and charges mobile
phones via USB-C connection. It includes an integrated battery pack for
storing energy when sunlight is not available.

Key Technical Features:
1. Foldable photovoltaic panel array using flexible or rigid mono/polycrystalline
   cells enclosed in a hinged weatherproof case
2. Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) charge controller for optimal energy
   harvesting across variable sunlight conditions
3. Integrated lithium-ion or LiFePO4 battery pack (10,000–26,800 mAh range)
   for energy storage when sunlight is unavailable
4. USB-C Power Delivery (PD) output port supporting up to 65W fast charging
5. USB-A legacy output port for simultaneous device charging
6. LED charge status indicator and battery level display
7. Over-voltage, over-current, and short-circuit protection circuitry

Expected Patent Landscape:
- High patent density area — solar portable chargers are a mature category
- Expected assignees: Anker, Goal Zero, BioLite, RAVPower, Jackery, Renogy
- Relevant CPC codes: H02J7/35, H01L31/042, H02S10/30, H02J7/00
